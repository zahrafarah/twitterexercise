Eminem killed it at Austin City Limits

EMINEM IS A RAP GOD, according to Twitter. At this year's Austin City Limits, concert goers who used Eminem in their tweet aslo used the hashtag #rapgod.

One person tweeted they felt bad for other artists, who preformed for empty crowds because people were waiting in line to see Eminem.

"Felt bad for @IGGYAZALEA at #acl right before @Eminem so alot of fans missed her waiting for a good spot to watch the #RapGod."

The people who couldn't see him, also went on Twitter to express their frustration.

"Megan was showing me a video of Eminem from ACL and all I could say was "I didn't see Eminem" and I burst straight into tears.. #obsession."
